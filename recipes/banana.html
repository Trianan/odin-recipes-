Banana

Description:

Get your potassium here! Bananananananananananananana...

Ingredients:
  • 1/4 pound bananas (or more).
Steps:
  1. Hold banana in hand, step facing down.
  2. Pinch end of banana hard.
  3. Peel downward; the tasty morsel is revealed!
  4. Eat with your mouth parts.
  5. Grab another banana, see step 1.
